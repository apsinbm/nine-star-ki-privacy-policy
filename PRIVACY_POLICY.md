# Privacy Policy for Nine Star Ki App

**Last Updated:** November 3, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Nine Star Ki mobile application ("the App," "we," or "us") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparency about our data practices.

## 1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The Nine Star Ki App is designed with privacy as a priority. We **do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information**, including but not limited to:

- ❌ Your name, email address, or phone number
- ❌ Your birth date (after calculation, it remains only on your device)
- ❌ Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or tracking data
- ❌ Location information or GPS data
- ❌ Camera, microphone, or contact list data
- ❌ Health or fitness information
- ❌ Browsing history or usage patterns
- ❌ Crash logs or diagnostic information sent to external servers

## 2. How the App Works

All calculations in the Nine Star Ki App are performed **locally on your device**:

1. You enter your birth date into the app
2. The app calculates your Nine Star Ki profile using a mathematical algorithm
3. Your results are displayed instantly on your device
4. No information is transmitted to any servers or third parties
5. Your data is not stored in any cloud or external database

## 3. No Third-Party Services

The Nine Star Ki App **does not use**:

- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
- Advertising networks (Google AdMob, Facebook Audience Network, etc.)
- Tracking libraries or SDKs
- Crash reporting services
- Data brokers or marketing platforms
- Social media integrations that share data

## 4. Local Storage

The Nine Star Ki App may store limited data locally on your device through standard iOS mechanisms:

- App preferences and settings
- Calculated profile data (optional, if you choose to save it)

This data is:
- Stored **only on your device**
- **Never transmitted** to any external server
- **Under your complete control** - you can delete the app at any time to remove all stored data
- Not accessible by Apple, our company, or any third parties

## 5. App Permissions

The Nine Star Ki App requests minimal permissions:

- **Calendar Access:** Not used. You manually enter your birth date.
- **Location Access:** Not requested or used.
- **Contacts:** Not requested or used.
- **Camera/Microphone:** Not requested or used.
- **Health Data:** Not requested or used.

## 6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- You can use the app without creating an account
- You can delete the app at any time to remove all local data
- You do not need to provide any personal information
- No data is shared with us or any third parties

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Nine Star Ki App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not:

- Knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
- Use behavioral advertising that targets children
- Request sensitive personal data from any user

The app is suitable for users of all ages (4+) and contains no objectionable content.

## 8. Security

We implement industry-standard practices to protect any local data on your device:

- All data remains on your device and is never transmitted over the internet
- No cloud synchronization means no data transmission risk
- Standard iOS security protections apply to the app

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Continued use of the App following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.

## 10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact:

**Email:** info@atlanticground.com

We will respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days.

## Summary

**Bottom Line:** The Nine Star Ki App respects your privacy by design. We don't collect, track, or store any personal information. All calculations happen on your device, and no data is ever transmitted to external servers.

Your trust is important to us, and we're committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy protection.
